Week 7 (9/15 - 9/19): Ethics & Professionalism Current Week
DayIn Class Plan
Mon 9/15 What Does Professionalism Mean for Engineers?
TEKS: 1A, 3D
Unit: Ethics & Professionalism in Engineering
  • Mini-lesson: What does professionalism mean for engineers?
  • Brainstorm: "3 ways to be professional on a robotics team" - Teams record notes in notebooks
  • Build time: Organize workspace, review robot progress
We Will: Define professionalism and identify how it applies to robotics.
I Will: Record three professional behaviors in my team notebook.
EQ: Why does professionalism matter for robotics teams?
Tue 9/16 What is Engineering Ethics?
TEKS: 1A
Focus: Ethical responsibilities in engineering work
  • Mini-lesson: What is engineering ethics?
  • Case scenario: Bending rules in scoring
  • Team activity: Teams write one example of a potential ethical issue
  • Build time: Continue robot design & coding
We Will: Discuss ethical responsibilities engineers have in their work.
I Will: Identify one ethical situation that could impact my robotics team.
EQ: How do ethics guide engineers in making responsible decisions?
Wed 9/17 Effective Teamwork & Communication
TEKS: 3D
Focus: Creating successful team agreements
  • Mini-lesson: Role-play poor vs. good team communication
  • Team activity: Teams draft Teamwork Agreements
  • Build time: Apply agreements while working on robot
We Will: Identify effective teamwork behaviors and create a team agreement.
I Will: Contribute to my team's teamwork agreement.
EQ: What makes teamwork successful in engineering projects?
Thu 9/18 Professionalism & Ethics at Competitions
TEKS: 1A, 3D
Focus: Competition behavior and team representation
  • Mini-lesson: How professionalism & ethics show up at competitions (safety, respect, helping others)
  • Notebook entry: "How professionalism and ethics apply to our team at competition"
  • Build time: Subsystem testing & troubleshooting
We Will: Connect professionalism and ethics to robotics competitions.
I Will: Write how professionalism and ethics apply to me at competition.
EQ: How do professionalism and ethics affect how others see our team at competition?
Fri 9/19 Team Reflection Assessment
Assessment: Team Reflection (Mastery Grade)
TEKS: 1A, 3D, 5D
  • Reflection prompt: "Why do professionalism & ethics matter to ME?"
  • Assessment: Students complete Team Reflection (graded)
  • Share-out: Quick share-out from each team
  • Build time: Open session for robot progress
We Will: Reflect on the importance of ethics and professionalism in engineering.
I Will: Explain one way I will demonstrate professionalism and ethics in my role.
EQ: How do professionalism and ethics help us succeed as individuals and as a team?
