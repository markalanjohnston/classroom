Ethics & Professionalism in Engineering + Teamwork Expectations

Monday, Sep 15

Mini-lesson:

What does professionalism mean for engineers?

Brainstorm:

"3 ways to be professional on a robotics team."

Teams record notes in notebooks.

Build time:

Organize workspace, review robot progress.

We Will: Define professionalism and identify how it applies to robotics.
I Will: Record three professional behaviors in my team notebook.
EQ: Why does professionalism matter for robotics teams?

Tuesday, Sep 16

Mini-lesson:

What is engineering ethics?

Case scenario:

Bending rules in scoring.

Teams write one example of a potential ethical issue.

Build time:

Continue robot design & coding.

We Will: Discuss ethical responsibilities engineers have in their work.
I Will: Identify one ethical situation that could impact my robotics team.
EQ: How do ethics guide engineers in making responsible decisions?

Wednesday, Sep 17

Mini-lesson:

Role-play poor vs. good team communication.

Activity:

Teams draft Teamwork Agreements.

Build time:

Apply agreements while working on robot.

We Will: Identify effective teamwork behaviors and create a team agreement.
I Will: Contribute to my team's teamwork agreement.
EQ: What makes teamwork successful in engineering projects?

Thursday, Sep 18

Mini-lesson:

How professionalism & ethics show up at competitions (safety, respect, helping others).

Notebook entry:

Teams write notebook entries: "How professionalism and ethics apply to our team at competition."

Build time:

Subsystem testing & troubleshooting.

We Will: Connect professionalism and ethics to robotics competitions.
I Will: Write how professionalism and ethics apply to me at competition.
EQ: How do professionalism and ethics affect how others see our team at competition?

Friday, Sep 19

Reflection prompt:

"Why do professionalism & ethics matter to ME?"

Assessment:

Students complete Team Reflection (graded).

Quick share-out from each team.

Build time:

Open session for robot progress.

We Will: Reflect on the importance of ethics and professionalism in engineering.
I Will: Explain one way I will demonstrate professionalism and ethics in my role.
EQ: How do professionalism and ethics help us succeed as individuals and as a team?
