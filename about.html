About Me

I'm a STEM and CTE educator specializing in Engineering, Robotics, and Computer Science. As a veteran teacher and program builder, I have a proven track record of launching competitive robotics teams, growing enrollment, and taking students to VEX Worlds.

My diverse background spans the U.S. Air Force, finance, corporate training, and K–12 leadership. This unique combination of experiences allows me to bring real-world perspectives into the classroom, helping students connect their learning to future careers and opportunities.

I'm passionate about creating innovative learning experiences that inspire students to explore STEM fields and develop the critical thinking, problem-solving, and collaboration skills they need for success.
