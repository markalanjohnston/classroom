⚖ Texas State Law

Pursuant to Texas House Bill 1481 (89th Legislature) and Texas Education Code §37.082, all school systems are required to adopt and implement a policy prohibiting the use of personal communication devices by students on school property during the instructional day. "Personal communication devices" includes cell phones, tablets, smartwatches, and any other device capable of telecommunication or digital communication.

In addition to state law, Mr. Johnston, as the teacher assigned to Room D156, exercises his prerogative to disallow phones and personal electronic devices at any time—not solely during school hours. This policy is in effect from the moment you enter this classroom until the moment you leave.
