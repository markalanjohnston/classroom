About Me

I teach Principles of Applied Engineering and W.I.N. Robotics at Hornedo. I focus on hands-on learning, clear routines, and real world engineering skills. Students will practice design thinking, iterate on prototypes, document their work, and learn to communicate results like young engineers.

You can read more about my background, teaching approach, and expectations here: Read More.
