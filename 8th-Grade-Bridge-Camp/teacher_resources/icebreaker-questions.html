Icebreaker Questions

For the ball-toss circle (or any "go around" moment)

How to use: Toss the ball — whoever catches it says their name and answers a question (their right thumb's spot, or just pick the next one on this list). Then they toss it on. Keep it light and fast. "Pass" is always allowed.
  1. What's your name and which middle school are you coming from?
  2. If you could have any superpower, what would it be?
  3. What's your go-to snack?
  4. Favorite video game right now?
  5. Cats, dogs, or something else?
  6. What's something you're really good at?
  7. Best movie or show you've watched lately?
  8. If you could travel anywhere, where would you go?
  9. What's your favorite school subject (be honest!)?
  10. Pancakes or waffles?
  11. What's a hobby you'd love to try?
  12. What's the best gift you've ever gotten?
  13. Would you rather explore space or the ocean?
  14. What song could you listen to on repeat?
  15. What's your favorite thing to do in the summer?
  16. If you could meet anyone, who would it be?
  17. What's a food you could eat every day?
  18. Do you have a hidden talent?
  19. What are you most excited about for high school?
  20. Would you rather time-travel to the past or the future?
  21. What's the coolest thing you've built or made?
  22. Favorite emoji?
  23. What's something that always makes you laugh?
  24. If you started a YouTube channel, what would it be about?
  25. What's your dream job right now?
  26. Beach day or mountain day?
  27. What's a word you love saying?
  28. What's something new you want to learn this year?
  29. Who's someone you look up to?
  30. If you could be a robot for a day, what would you do first?
