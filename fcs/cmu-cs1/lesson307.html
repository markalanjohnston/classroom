Class 307 – Unit 1 Creative Mini-Task

Design and build your own original program

Today's Goals

  • Design and build your own original CMU CS Academy program
  • Use the skills from Unit 1 in a creative way
  • Explain your thinking through a short reflection

Agenda

  • Introduction to the Creative Mini-Task
  • Review rubric and expectations
  • Plan your idea
  • Code your project
  • Write reflection

What You Must Finish Today

  • Create an original drawing program in CMU CS Academy
  • Use Unit 1 skills: shapes, colors, and properties
  • Write a short reflection about your project

Homework

Finish your Creative Mini-Task and reflection if not completed in class

Quick Reminder

  • Start with a plan before coding
  • Build your project step by step
  • Creativity matters, but meeting the rubric matters more

Grading Rubric (Student Version)

Category What You Need
Program Design – 30% Clear purpose and good use of Unit 1 concepts
Program Development – 35% Explain a challenge you faced and how you solved it
Reflection – 35% Describe at least two Unit 1 concepts used in your project
Bonus Extra creativity and originality
