Quick Study Tips
  • Three materials: Metals (inorganic), Ceramics (inorganic), Polymers (organic)
  • Raw material methods: Drilling, Mining, Harvesting (all of the above)
  • Primary processing: Raw materials → Standard stock (lumber, ingots, bars, rods)
  • Secondary processing: Standard stock → Useful products (4 techniques)
  • Four techniques: Separating (cutting), Casting/Molding (liquid), Forming (bending), Combining (welding)
  • Pouring concrete = Casting/Molding (NOT forming)
  • Hammering steel = Forming (compression, bending, stretching)
  • Custom: Single/small products | Intermittent: Small batches | Continuous: Highest volume, lowest cost
  • Process layout: Based on kind of work | Product layout: Based on product sequence
  • Jigs guide tools | Fixtures hold parts
  • Specification sheets include every detail of a part
  • Flow process charts record order of manufacturing processes
  • Time & motion studies increase worker productivity
  • Modern plastics made from petroleum
  • Worker training & motivation key to quality control success
  • Seed tree cutting leaves SOME trees (NOT all trees)
