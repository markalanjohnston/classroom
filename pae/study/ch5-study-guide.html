Quick Study Tips
  • Remember the sketching steps order: Visualize → Block out → Outline → Detail
  • Historical research = looking at past solutions; Experimental = testing new ideas
  • Trade-offs mean gaining one quality often means losing another
  • Cost feasibility specifically refers to financial evaluation
  • Materials have properties that must be tested and considered
