Course Overview

W.I.N. Robotics introduces students to programming, problem-solving, and engineering through the VEX platform. Students will:

  • Learn block-based programming with VEX VR
  • Transition to Python programming
  • Build and program physical robots
  • Complete team challenges and competitions
  • Develop engineering notebooks
