Unit 3: Intro to CMU Graphics

Students design their first program in Python with CMU Graphics while learning both fundamental programming concepts and collaborative software development processes (CRD, AAP). Students work to develop this simple program that showcases something of personal interest (CTP1, CTP4, CTP6). Throughout the unit they learn how to use CMU Graphics to design graphical programs. Along the way, students learn practices like debugging, pair programming, and collecting and responding to feedback, which they will be able to use throughout the course as they build ever more complex projects.
